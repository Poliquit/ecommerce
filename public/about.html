What We Support

OUR MISSION

To build a bridge between the world and all Filipino creators.

Our goal is for our platform to become the go-to place on the internet for people looking to buy handmade goods made by Filipino artists. By giving these artists all the resources and assistance they need, we hope to further elevate the art business while also making it easier for them to succeed in domestic and global marketplaces.

OUR VISION

A global community that supports Filipino artists.

A world that prospers for Filipino artists. Acknowledging himself as the acknowledged worldwide expert, Hiyas imagines a transnational Filipino art scene that is appreciated and well-known, creating a rich cultural tapestry that transcends national boundaries.
